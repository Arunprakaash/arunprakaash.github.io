About

During my studies and early career, I developed a strong interest in AI and machine learning applications, which I’ve been able to explore through various professional experiences.

I had the opportunity to work as an AI Researcher/Engineer and a Founding Member at Sayvai, and as an AI/ML Intern at Bosch Global Software Technologies.

Outside work, I’m a hobby game developer who created “Math Craze,” a puzzle game on Play Store. Some of my works at Artstation. Passionate about AI innovation, I’m currently exploring Reinforcement Learning and LLMs.
